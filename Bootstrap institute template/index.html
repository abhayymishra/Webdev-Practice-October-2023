SSGI is rated among the top educational and professional Group of India . Established in 1970s, SSGI is one of the first self-financing Groups providing education and professional training in the region. The group has already celebrated its Silver Jubilee and every year attracts a diverse student community from all over India. SSGI is known for its rich, vibrant campus life marked by technical, cultural and sports events running throughout the year.
